HATCH ACCELERATOR

entrepreneurship@UBC's HATCH Accelerator Program provides you and your team the support, space and expertise to take your world-changing innovation to the next level.

The accelerator is for ventures who have completed the Incubator Program and been invited to continue their venture-building journey with entrepreneurship@UBC by joining the HATCH Accelerator.

Intake - January and July

Lab2Launch

entrepreneurship@UBC’s Lab2Launch venture building program is for UBC researchers and scientists who are focusing on the development of a scientific or technological innovation which is unique, proprietary and difficult to reproduce, propelling them through venture creation, team building, investment and growth.

Application Due Date - August 10th

CORE

entrepreneurship@UBC’s CORE program is for ventures driving innovation through disruptive technologies, ideas and trends. In CORE, you’ll focus on achieving product-market fit and preparing for investment and growth.

Application Due Date - August 10th

Social Venture

entrepreneurship@UBC’s Social Venture stream supports impact-driven entrepreneurs providing them with the tailored programming, mentorship and resources to help them succeed

Need to be a part of Lab2Launch or CORE

Intro to Entrepreneruship - COMM 280

Do you have what it takes to be an entrepreneur? Want to find out? In this hands-on interactive course, you'll learn about entrepreneurial essentials such as creating viable opportunities; building your founding team; strategic planning; going to market; fleshing out your organization; and looking ahead to the requirements of the future of the business, and your role within it.

Eligibility: This course is open to BCom students in Years 2 and 3, and non-BCom students in Years 2, 3, and 4.
