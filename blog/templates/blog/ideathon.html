What is An Ideathon?

An ideathon is a short, intensive, workshop-like experience for students to ideate creative applications using existing patents.

Previous Next

How does an Ideathon work?

  • Participants are provided with simplified versions of existing patented technologies
  • They are given 24 hours to come up with creative applications(products or services) using those patents
  • The most innovative and realistic products win
